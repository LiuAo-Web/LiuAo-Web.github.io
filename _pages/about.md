I am a PhD candidate in Municipal Engineering at [Dalian University of Technology](https://www.dlut.edu.cn/) and currently a visiting doctoral researcher in urban transport at the [University of Tokyo](https://www.u-tokyo.ac.jp/en/). 

My recent work examines how pricing, infrastructure, and spatial structure shape the environmental and social impacts of emerging mobility systems. I combine transportation modeling, optimization, geospatial analysis, and environmental assessment to study shared autonomous vehicles, electric bus charging systems, and urban air quality.

**学术成果**：在*Sustainable Cities and Society*, *Applied Energy*, *npj Urban Sustainability*, *Cities*等国际和国内交通与城市规划领域顶级/主流期刊上发表论文8篇，其中被SCI/SSCI/EI收录7篇（**中科院1区TOP期刊4篇**），主要成果被大工官网转载报道。

**科研获奖**：成果获钱学森城市学（交通）金奖提名奖、辽宁省大学生交通科技大赛二等奖等奖励。

研究兴趣
======
- 共享自动驾驶汽车与城市可持续发展
- 逻辑驱动的多源交通大数据分析
- 土地利用和交通整合规划
- 机器学习与交通系统优化
