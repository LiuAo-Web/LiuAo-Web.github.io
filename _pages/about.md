I am a PhD candidate in Municipal Engineering at [Dalian University of Technology](https://www.dlut.edu.cn/) and currently a visiting doctoral researcher in urban transport at the [University of Tokyo](https://www.u-tokyo.ac.jp/en/). My research focuses on shared autonomous vehicles, integrated land use-transport planning, accessibility, and urban sustainability.

My recent work examines how pricing, infrastructure, and spatial structure shape the environmental and social impacts of emerging mobility systems. I combine transportation modeling, optimization, geospatial analysis, and environmental assessment to study shared autonomous vehicles, electric bus charging systems, and urban air quality.

I have published eight peer-reviewed papers, including articles in *Sustainable Cities and Society*, *Applied Energy*, *Cities*, and *npj Urban Sustainability*. I also contributed to the Springer book *Logic-Driven Traffic Big Data Analytics: Methodology and Applications for Planning*.

Research interests
======
- Shared autonomous vehicles and mobility pricing
- Land use-transport interaction and accessibility
- Electric bus systems and charging infrastructure
- Urban air quality, energy, and sustainability analytics
